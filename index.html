Who I am

Hello! Thanks for coming to visit my site. Let's introduce myself. My name is Prasen Gope. I'm a QA Automation Engineer by profession focusing on Java-based Selenium tool to script automation test-cases. But I was always eager to jump into the world of software development. To materialize that dream, I got admitted into the UNC Chapel Hill's FULL STACK FLEX program to become a full stack developer.

Bootcamp Experience

This has been quite a journey so far with the bootcamp! There were ups and downs with homework or projects. But the excitement of discovering the inner world of software development is what kept me going. Completing a project by calling the APIs, using a simple Bootstrap class instead of a few lines of CSS codes, being able to prompt user to input using the Inquirer package on Node.js are just some of the examples of pure joy.

Goal

My short-term goal is to get a job as a backend developer upon successful completion of the course to get the real-world taste of coding. I'm willing to relocate anywhere. But in longer term, within 5 years from now, I want to see myself as a freelancer running a BPO business possibly collaborating with friends.

Life outside coding

Outside of coding, I do have a 'regular' life as well. I'm a father to a wonderful 3 year old son and husband to a wonderful charming lady. Spending time with them is what I look forward to every day. Photography is my another weakness. I'm not a professional photographer by the way. I also like to watch good movies and listen to books on Audible.
